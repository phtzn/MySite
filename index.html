About Me

I was born and raised in the Philippines and my mother migrated us to the United States in 2017. Although I was culturally shocked, there's tons of opportunities to explore.

Drawing has been my forever passion and I wanted to push it to the next level. When I came into the states, I didn't know I can start my career off with my artistic skills. Sadly, it didn't start right away and worked in retail for three years. I still work in customer service but in the fulfillment department for Aceable and I am loving it!

I love everything technology and the trends that come with it. Since then, my bitter dislike for terrible website and app design was born. I never knew about UX/UI but it met all things that I love and hate.

​

I have also pushed myself to exceed in expressing my creativity and I have been fascinated with how cars are built and modified. I had a stanced 2017 Mazda 3 that motivated me to build a 1983 Mazda Rx7 from the ground up.

I have been focusing on designing logos and I pushed myself to attend Rice University's UX/UI Bootcamp to pursue my dreams and jump start my career!
